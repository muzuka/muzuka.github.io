Hello! I'm Sean Brown, a software developer specializing in games and VR. I live in Calgary, Canada.
I've been working for the past six years as a VR developer working on all kinds of awesome games and simulations. Before that I worked on games and apps as a freelancer. I love supporting the Alberta game development scene and I owe a lot to them. I was on the board of the Calgary Game Developers Association for two years.
I'm really proud of all the games I've helped make and made myself. I've worked on multiplayer VR games, FPS games, and even an MMO briefly. I love teaching and have taught kids game design and web design. I even taught a VR bootcamp at SAIT. (That was crazy but fun!)
