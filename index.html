Hi, I’m Sean Brown, a software developer based in Calgary, Canada with a focus on Unity, VR, and multiplayer systems. Over the past six years, I’ve built immersive VR simulations, online shooters, and educational games, and before that I worked as a freelancer on mobile and WebGL projects, often handling full-stack development. I’m passionate about the Alberta game dev scene, having served on the board of the Calgary Game Developers Association and taught everything from web/game design at the library to VR at SAIT. I enjoy tackling complex technical challenges and crafting polished, interactive experiences across platforms.
