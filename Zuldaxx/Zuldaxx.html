Zuldaxx

2019

FPS | Multiplayer | Battle Royale | Unity

Role: Lead Unity Developer

A first person shooter battle royale set in Calgary, Alberta

I led the development of this game implementing the gameplay mechanics and the multiplayer systems including matchmaking. I worked with artists and level designers to complete the project.

Players choose a character and a level inspired by real places and fight for survival while a deadly storm slowly closes in. Explore the map to find guns, healing, and even portals. The last player standing wins.
