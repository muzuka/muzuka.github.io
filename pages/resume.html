More about me

I am an independent game developer based in Calgary. I have a strong passion for designing and programming video games and have strived for excellence in computer graphics. I have been using Unity for the last year and have gained a lot of experience through game jams and my current project Beach Wars that is in development.
I am a member of the Calgary Game Developers Association. A group dedicated to connecting and supporting game developers in Calgary. I am also a member of Team Zeus's motor controller team. Team Zeus is an electric motorcycle team based at the University of Calgary.
