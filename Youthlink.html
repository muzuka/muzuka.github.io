Role: Lead Unity Developer

Museum presentation app for facilitating presentations in a classroom setting.

I led the development of this app including client management, technical development, and final delivery. The app consisted of five minigames designed to be played as a group and two separate applications that fulfill two roles. It has a presenter application for creating and facilitating interactive presentations, customizing game content, and viewing presentation analytics. The audience application offers two modes: a public mode for free gameplay and a presentation mode where users engage with content controlled by the presenter. Both applications are designed to find each other in a closed network via UDP pings and TCP connections so in only a few seconds a presentation can be started in a classroom.
